Guides

Below are a few guides to help you use some of the sites functions.

Signing Up

  1. Find the "new patient form" located at the bottom right of the homepage of the website. (Alternatively, there is a "register" button at the top of each page that isn't the home page.)
  2. Enter your user information into the form.
  3. Click "Sign up". You will then be forwarded to your patient dashboard.

Logging In

  1. Find the "Login" button located at the top right of any page of the website.
  2. Enter your user name in the designated text box.
  3. Enter your password in the designated text box.
  4. Click "Sign In". You will then be forwarded to your patient dashboard.

Updating Your Profile

  1. Follow the steps from "Logging In" above to login to your user account.
  2. On the patient dashboard, you will find a button labeled "Update Profile". Click it.
  3. Once you click the update profile button you will be sent to the update profile page.
  4. Make the appropriate changes to your profile here. Note: the fields are pre-populated with your information, so you only need to fill in what you want to update.
  5. Once you are satisfied with your updates, click the "Update" button.
